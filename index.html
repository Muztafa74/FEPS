What is the cost of education and are there opportunities for scholarships?

The Faculty of Economics and political science provides many scholarships for outstanding students with a high GPA, whether in terms of tuition fees or studying abroad, for more details click here.

Is there support available for international students?

Yes, there is support provided for Palestinian, Sudanese and Syrian students, where they pay 50% only of the amount of their tuition fees, according to an official decision.

How to evaluate the academic performance of students?

Through the credit hours system, calculating the GPA according to the number of hours prescribed for each subject being studied.

Are there opportunities for Academic Exchange or study abroad?

Yes, through international partnerships and cooperation agreements between the college and globally corresponding colleges, the college provides many opportunities for exchange for a semester or more in Foreign universities. for more.

Our Popular Courses in Major SSC

Electronic government systems

The target of this course is to lay the foundations of e-government and e-politics as an emerging interdisciplinary area of research and practice, and stressing the potential offered by the Internet for a greater interactivity between the public and political spheres.

Applied information systems

This course teaches students about the roles of applied information systems (IS) in modern business societies, their basic concepts, components, life cycle, main types (transaction processing, management, decision support, expert, geographic, web-based), and successful examples of IS in business applications.

Social system theory

The goal of this course is to introduce students to the area of systems theory. The proposition of systems theory is that a system's behavior is the result of its own structure. The internal structure of a system drives its behavior. It is a way of thinking that allows us to identify the root causes of problems.

Database management systems

In this course, you will learn: What is a database?How can you design/develop one? How can you save data in a database? How can you extract information from a database? What are the meanings of the related "fancy" words like data warehouse, datamining, structured vs unstructured data, business analytics, business intelligence, big data, etc.
